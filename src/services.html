What is a russian manicure?

Unlike the conventional manicure most of us are familiar with, the Russian manicure delves into meticulousness. Originating from Russia, this technique swaps the traditional cuticle clippers for electric drills with various attachments, ensuring every nook and cranny is accounted for.

What is the difference between a gel manicure and a Russian manicure?

While a gel manicure is centered on the type of polish and its lasting finish, a Russian manicure emphasizes meticulous cuticle care and preparation of the nail. They can be combined, however: one could get a Russian manicure to prep the nails and then have a gel polish applied for a long-lasting, polished look.

This russian manicure (without colour) service offers professional nail care. The service includes nail shaping, buffing, cuticle treatment, with the option to add clear nail strengthener, cuticle oil and hand cream.

Gel Manicure From $80

This service includes nail shaping, buffing, cuticle care, and gel polish application in any color of your choice. Using high-quality brands, we ensure a long-lasting, flawless finish. Hard Gel From $115

This treatment includes nail shaping, buffing, and cuticle care, while strengthening the nails using hard gel. Perfect for creating a smooth, durable finish for short, medium, or long nails. Hard gel provides superior strength and can correct curved or downward-growing nails, offering long-lasting results. Unlike BIAB or Bottle Gel, hard gel is much stronger and ideal for clients needing extra durability.

The technique: precision at its finest

1

Preparation

Nails are first cleaned and shaped. It's not just about aesthetic; ensuring nails are of even length can prevent future breakages.

2

Softening

A special cuticle softener is applied, which prepares the nail bed for the detailed work ahead.

3

Drill Time

Electric drills gently buff away dead skin, clean the nail plate, and polish, leaving nails smooth and ready for polish or gel.

4

Polishing

With nails perfectly prepped, any polish—nude, French tip, or glitter—looks stunning.

5

Finishing Touches

A hydrating cuticle oil is applied, ensuring the skin remains nourished and supple, adding the finishing touch to this luxury experience.

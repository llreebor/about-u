Our hygiene commitment

Autoclave Sterilization: Ensuring Total Safety

Our tools are meticulously sterilized using an autoclave, a medical-grade device that eliminates bacteria, viruses, and other pathogens. By adhering to this industry-standard procedure, we provide you with a clean, safe environment where health is a top priority.

Personal Protective Equipment: Your Safety, Our Priority

Our team wears gloves and masks during every service, minimizing any risk of cross-contamination. This protective gear not only keeps our staff safe but also creates a safe and worry-free environment for you to enjoy your service with peace of mind.

Single-Use Files: Dedicated to Your Health

To eliminate any possibility of cross-contamination, we use a fresh, single-use file for each client. This step reflects our commitment to the highest hygiene standards, offering you a uniquely safe and personalized experience.
